Why apply?

Joining a season means becoming part of a world shaped by real players. Every applicant has the chance to influence diplomacy, borders, leadership, conflict, and the overall history of the server.

Enter a living world

Step into a season where the world changes every second, whether it be war, diplomacy or even a new government.
