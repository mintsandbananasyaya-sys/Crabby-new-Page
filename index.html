What this server is about

Crabby's Civilization Experiments is a player-driven civilization server built around politics, expansion, alliances, conflict, survival, and the stories that appear when real people shape a shared world.

Player-made nations

Every season gives players the chance to create governments, cultures, alliances, rivalries, and empires that matter.

Emergent history

The best moments are unscripted: major wars, sudden collapses, betrayals, diplomacy, and strange power shifts.

Different every season

Each experiment can change the map, balance of power, rules, themes, and how civilization develops.
